# Transformation Mastery Blueprint

This blueprint follows the character creation pass. Species now defines identity and affinity; forms now need a progression layer that rewards long-term use.

## Five Stages

1. Form catalog and unlock routes.
2. Mastery XP, ranks, drain scaling, and perks.
3. Species affinities and form behavior.
4. Forms UI and save compatibility.
5. Documentation and validation.

## Forms

- Base: stable recovery and universal technique access.
- Flare: early pressure form unlocked from Ravik.
- Storm: weather/blast form unlocked from Mara Coil or Storm Seal.
- Iron Spirit: endurance form unlocked from Thunder Warden.
- Solar Apex: late-game radiant power form.
- Eclipse Break: forbidden high-risk branch form.

## Mastery Rules

- Active transformed time grants mastery.
- Hits and victories grant extra mastery.
- Boss victories grant the largest mastery rewards.
- Mastery ranks reduce drain and reveal stronger perks.
- Species affinities can improve mastery speed, damage, guard, or drain for certain forms.

## Species Affinities

- Starforged favors Flare.
- Bio-Arc favors Storm.
- Verdant favors Iron Spirit.
- Majinite favors Eclipse Break.
- Android stabilizes form drain and guard.
- Celestial improves general form control.
- Terran remains the flexible hybrid baseline.

## Save Compatibility

Form mastery is stored on `player.formMastery` and backfilled for older saves through compatibility defaults.
